How to use (tap to expand)
Tap any action button below. Your native SMS app will launch with the recipient number and system triggers pre-filled. For Incident Logs, type your text in the box before hitting the button.

Always review the message before sending to ensure accuracy. This tool is designed for quick communication with SARCall and team groups, but should be used responsibly and in accordance with team protocols.
